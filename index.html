About Ufarms

Professional garden and landscape solutions

Ufarms Agritech Pvt Ltd is an India-based irrigation technology company focused on delivering smart, efficient, and reliable solutions for landscape and drip irrigation.

With a strong foundation in agricultural engineering and automation, we design and deploy systems that help conserve water, reduce manual effort, and improve crop and landscape health.

We serve farms, estates, gardens, parks, and commercial landscapes with tailor-made irrigation setups that are easy to install, maintain, and scale.

Driven by innovation and practical field experience, Ufarms Agritech brings modern irrigation technologies to everyday growers and landscape professionals across India.

Drip Irrigation & Automation

Water-efficient systems with smart technology

Water-Efficient Drip Irrigation & Automation

Maximize your garden's health while minimizing water usage with our advanced drip irrigation systems. Our precision installations deliver water directly to plant roots, reducing waste and promoting deeper root growth. We integrate smart controllers and sensors that automatically adjust watering schedules based on weather conditions, soil moisture, and plant needs. Control everything through our user-friendly mobile app, allowing you to monitor and manage your garden's irrigation from anywhere. Our systems are customizable for any garden size, from small residential gardens to large commercial landscapes.
